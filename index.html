What is HTML?

Before diving into the world of HTML and beginning our coding venture, it is imperative to understand what HTML is. HTML stands for Hypertext Markup Language, the language that makes the World Wide Web possible. HTML was invented by Tim Berners Lee in 1989. A markup language formats text, such that when displayed, the markeups do not appear in HTML, the markups are called tags and are shown with the angle brackets, or < and >.
